# Frequently Asked Questions

## When does the next intake start?
New intakes begin at the start of every month.

## Do you offer evening or weekend classes?
Yes, the Digital Marketing certificate runs in the evening. Other programs offer part-time options on request.

## Is there a payment plan?
Yes, all programs support installment payments as described in the fees information.

## What are the entry requirements?
A high school certificate is required for all diploma programs. No prior experience is needed for the Digital Marketing certificate.
